How to Subscribe Manually

For Apple Calendar (iCalendar):

  1. Open Calendar app on your Mac or iOS device.
  2. Go to File > New Calendar Subscription (Mac) or tap the Calendars tab, then Add Account > Add Subscribed Calendar (iOS).
  3. Paste the ICS URL from above.
  4. Click Subscribe.
  5. Choose update frequency (e.g., daily) and click OK.

Note: If it fails, download the .ics file first, then double-click it to open in Calendar.

For Google Calendar:

  1. Open Google Calendar in your browser.
  2. On the left sidebar, click the + next to "Other calendars" → From URL.
  3. Paste the ICS URL from above.
  4. Click Add Calendar.
  5. The events will now appear in your Google Calendar.

Note: If the URL doesn't work, download the .ics file by clicking "Copy ICS Path" and then "Download", then in Google Calendar go to Settings > Import & export > Choose file and upload it.
